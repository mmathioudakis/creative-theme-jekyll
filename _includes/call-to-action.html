About the course

The University of Helsinki offers a MSc program in Data Science that organizes a Data Science Project course. During the course, students work on practical data science projects in small groups, providing prototype data science products for external clients, such as companies or research organizations.

This form is used to collect project ideas from clients and serves as the initial contact point between the client and the course organizers.

What you get: A group of skilled and enthusiastic master's level students working on a project related to your interests. You also get in close contact with potential recruits.

What we expect: Every project should offer a possibility to work on real data, either provided by the client or available as open data. We also expect a responsible contact person who agrees to meet with the group 2-3 times during the course, to provide comments on their plans and final results.
